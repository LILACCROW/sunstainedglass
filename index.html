Love

You don't have to fit your relationships into "just friends" and "romantic partners". There isn't a rulebook that tells you to. You are an entire living being with an emotional range as large as life itself. Your brain is full of haywire signals and your body is full of rogue chemicals and hormones. You can't cram that experience into cookie cutters. you don't have to tame or fear what you cannot name.

Try instead...

  • be ok with people asking if you are queer (their interest extends only to whether or not they can kiss you)
  • complete vulnerability among friends
  • being messy and hungry and needy
  • saying yes
  • breaking your imaginary rules at all costs
  • letting others break your imaginary rules without shame
  • being violently aware and passionately supportive of people doing things you dont understand
